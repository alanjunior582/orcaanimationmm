Nausicaa: Valley Of The Wind (1984)

The film tells the story of Nausicaä (Shimamoto), the young princess of the Valley of the Wind.
She becomes embroiled in a struggle with Tolmekia, a kingdom that tries to use an ancient weapon to eradicate a jungle full of giant mutant insects.
