Tokyo Ghoul Season 2 (2016)

The season roughly adapts to the second half of the Tokyo Ghoul manga.
Unlike the first season, however, Tokyo Ghoul does not directly adapt everything from the manga.
The anime follows Ken Kaneki after he joins Aogiri Tree, as the group begins their battle against the CCG, who are trying to exterminate the ghoul organization.
