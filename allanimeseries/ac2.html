Assassination Classroom Season 2 (2015)

Earth is left in jeopardy after an enormously powerful tentacled creature suddenly appears and destroys 70% of the Moon, leaving it in the shape of a crescent.
The creature claims that within a year, he will destroy the planet next. However, he offers mankind a chance to avert this fate.
