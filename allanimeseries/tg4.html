Tokyo Ghoul Season 4 (2018)

After Furuta leaves his last breath, Kaneki goes to the core of the Dragon in order to destroy it only to find out that Rize's body was used as its energy source. After releasing her, the battle comes to an end.
Six years later, Tokyo has come back to normal with humans and ghouls living peacefully amongst each other.
